Our Project

SHELDON was an experiment in spontaneous community building. The initial supply of 1 Trillion Tokens was airdropped to anyone who signed up. Since then, 98% of the supply has been bought back and burnt.​

In November 2021 to help decentralize WOOF a community DAO was formed under the name of
WOOF DAO.
The DAO votes on the future of the project and all the DAO wallets are 100% transparent and available to the public domain.​


To help reverse the ongoing effects of animal abuse and neglect worldwide, the WOOF DAO donated $10,000 of BTC to 4 dog charities in need.

Get Started

1.

Create Coin98 or Phantom Wallet

Create a Coin98 or Phantom Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send and receive WOOF.

2.

Send SOL to your wallet

You can buy Solana (SOL) directly on Coin98 or transfer it to your Phantom Wallet from exchanges like FTX, Binance, Coinbase, etc. Make sure to use the SOL Network when transferring SOL.

3.


Connect your wallet to WoofSwap

Access your wallet to  SheldSwap by clicking "Connect to a wallet" and selecting Phantom or Coin98.

4.


Swap SOL for Sheldon

You can start swapping as soon as you have SOL available! Press "Select a token" and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
